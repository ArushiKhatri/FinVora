About FinVora

FinVora is a lightweight personal budgeting tool built to help you take control of your finances without the clutter of complex apps.

Add your income sources, log your expenses by category, set a monthly budget and savings goal — then let FinVora calculate where you stand. Every time you calculate, the summary is saved to your history so you can track trends month by month.

No ads. No subscriptions. Just simple, honest finance tracking.

What You Can Do

  • Add multiple income sources
  • Track expenses across 8 categories (Food, Transport, Bills, etc.)
  • Set a monthly budget and savings goal
  • Get a full summary — income, spending, balance, savings status
  • See which category you spend the most on
  • View month-wise history of all your calculations
